Ulysses

This is my cat Ulysses. He is incredibly needy and often joins me on Zoom calls. He travels really well and we regularly go on road trips around Idaho.

Homebrew Board Games

A few years ago I became very interested in board game design. Though I haven't brought any of my games to market yet, I love the wonderful combination of human psychology, UX design, and game theory required to build them.

Red Regime

Inspired by Armando Iannucci's 'The Death of Stalin', I built Red Regime for the 2019 Board Wars 48-Hour competition. As one of the few solo competitors, I was still able to win both the People's Choice and Best Design awards.

Since that time I have revised Red Regime three times and uploaded it on to Tabletop Simulator for playtesting.

Spire

My first game, Spire still remains my favorite. Based on competitive tower-building, developing Spire motivated me to learn how to build and print 3D designs.
